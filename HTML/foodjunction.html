Biryani

Biryani is a popular and flavorful rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is known for its aromatic blend of spices, fragrant basmati rice, and tender meat or vegetables. Biryani has many regional variations, and it is widely loved and prepared across South Asia and the Middle East.

Dosa

Dosa is a popular and versatile South Indian dish that has gained widespread popularity not only in India but also around the world. It is a thin, crispy pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram). Dosa is a staple in the southern regions of India, particularly in states like Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Kerala.
